# Dendera Zodiac

#: 4
Type / Medium: Sandstone bas-relief
Year (approx.): 50 BCE
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dendera_zodiac
Idea or Message: Hellenistic–Egyptian synthesis of cosmology and divination
Why It Is Significant: First complete ancient sky map in round form; influenced Greco-Roman astrology

Первая полная карта древнего неба в круглой форме; оказала влияние на греко-римскую астрологию

# Farnese Atlas

#: 6
Type / Medium: Marble sculpture
Year (approx.): 2nd C CE (copy of 2nd C BCE original)
Source: https://www.electrummagazine.com/2016/09/the-farnese-atlas/
Idea or Message: Embodies cosmos as burden of knowledge
Why It Is Significant: Oldest surviving 3-D star globe; physical bridge between myth and astronomy

Старейший из сохранившихся трехмерных звездных глобусов; физический мост между мифом и астрономией

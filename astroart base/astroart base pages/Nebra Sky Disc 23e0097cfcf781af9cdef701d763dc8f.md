# Nebra Sky Disc

#: 2
Type / Medium: Bronze disc inlaid with gold
Year (approx.): c. 1,800 BCE
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nebra_sky_disc
Idea or Message: Synchronizes lunar–solar calendars; blends empirical observation with myth
Why It Is Significant: Oldest concrete image of the cosmos on metal; rewrites Bronze-Age astronomical capability
